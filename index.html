Capital Markets Advisory

Engagement Pipeline
& Proposal Structure

Our capital advisory process is designed to quickly determine whether a transaction is viable for our network of investors and lenders, while protecting both parties through a structured review and proposal stage.

The process follows four clear stages

I — Initial Pipeline Entry
II — NDA & Review Deposit
III — Formal Advisory Proposal
IV — Advisory Engagement
I
Stage One

Initial Pipeline Entry

The first step is a simple intake conversation to determine whether the opportunity is aligned with our investor and lender network.

No Disclosure Mode

At this stage, both sides quickly determine if the deal is worth progressing without exposing sensitive information prematurely.

  • We review a high-level overview of the transaction
  • No confidential materials are shared
  • The opportunity remains in No Disclosure Mode

Stage Two

The Method

Why This Structure Works

This model ensures that capital opportunities are presented with both financial credibility and media visibility. The goal is to transform transactions into compelling investment opportunities that attract capital.

01
Efficient Deal Filtering

Deals are filtered quickly through a structured intake process, protecting everyone's time and ensuring only viable opportunities move forward.

02
Protected Confidentiality

Confidential information remains protected through our No Disclosure Mode and NDA process before any sensitive materials are reviewed.

03
Professional Positioning

Clients receive professional positioning and presentation development before approaching investors, maximizing credibility and first impressions.

04
Media-Amplified Capital

Capital opportunities are presented with media visibility and financial credibility together — a combination that differentiates deals in a competitive market.

Pricing Currency

Canadian clients are invoiced in CAD. U.S. clients are invoiced in USD. Pricing is equivalent across jurisdictions at current exchange rates.
